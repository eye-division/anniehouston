Hi. I’m Annie Houston, a London-based graphic designer.


Welcome           to my online portfolio.

I specialise in design for print, and generally work from conception to production on any given job: from mocking up scamps to creating print-ready artwork.

I am a born perfectionist, with a keen eye for detail and a love of colour and typography.

I’m a freelancer who enjoys working collaboratively, as part of a team as well as working solo and taking the lead on projects.

I’d love to meet your design needs.            Get in touch if you’d like to know my rates or have any other queries.

Enjoy your visit.
