Bravado / Universal Music mens outerwear range, autumn/winter 2008-2009, featuring artwork from classic rock and metal bands. These jackets were aimed at the higher end of the market, made from real leather with attention to detail, such as customised zip-pulls, lining and studs. These jackets were right on trend, launched at the peak of vintage and biker-chic trends. Sold through various high street retailers.
